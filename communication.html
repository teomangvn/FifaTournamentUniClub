Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the FIFA Club?

To join our club, you need to complete the registration form on our Registration page. After submitting the form, you'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

How much is the membership fee?

The membership fee is $20 per semester or $30 for the entire academic year. Payment can be made online or at our club office.

Can non-students participate in tournaments?

Currently, our tournaments are only open to registered students. However, we occasionally host special events that are open to everyone.

What equipment do I need to bring?

All gaming equipment is provided by the club. However, you're welcome to bring your own controller if you prefer.
