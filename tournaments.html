Tournament Rules

General Rules

  • All participants must be registered club members
  • Participants must arrive 15 minutes before their scheduled match
  • Standard FIFA game settings will be used
  • Match duration: 6 minutes per half
  • Tournament officials' decisions are final

Format Details

  • Group Stage: Round-robin format with 4 players per group
  • Points System: Win (3 points), Draw (1 point), Loss (0 points)
  • Knockout Stage: Single elimination matches
  • Finals: Best of 3 matches
